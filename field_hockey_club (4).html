Our Story
About The Club

Founded in 1994, Vipers Field Hockey Club has grown from a grassroots initiative into one of the most respected field hockey clubs in the region. Built on discipline, teamwork and relentless pursuit of excellence, we have consistently produced champions both on and off the pitch.

We welcome players of all levels — from beginners discovering the sport to elite athletes chasing titles. Our professional coaching staff, world-class facilities and winning culture set us apart.
